Dr. Oscar Novick z"l

אשר אנשל בן זלמן

1931 - 2020

Dr. Oscar Novick was a well-respected pediatrician, community leader, philanthropist, Torah-dedicated Jew and beloved husband, father, grandfather and great-grandfather. As an only child born to immigrant parents, he lived a full life dedicated to helping others through his medical expertise, kindness, leadership, and his service in the US Army. He leaves behind an inspiring legacy that will be carried on by his 4 children, 21 grandchildren, and 51 great-grand children (and counting) in the US and Israel. May his memory be for a blessing.
